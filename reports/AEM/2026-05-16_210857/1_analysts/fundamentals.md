## 1. COMPANY PROFILE

## 8. RISK FACTORS

1. **Gold Price Sensitivity**: AEM's fortunes are tightly linked to gold prices. The margin expansion story could reverse if gold prices decline materially.

2. **Cost Inflation**: While margins have expanded, cost of revenue has grown from $3.87B (FY 2022) to $4.99B (FY 2025). Sustained cost increases could pressure margins if gold prices stagnate.

3. **Geopolitical/Mining Risk**: Operations span Canada, Sweden, and Finland — generally stable jurisdictions, but mining carries inherent operational risks.

4. **CapEx Intensity**: Capital expenditures have nearly doubled from $1.54B (FY 2022) to $2.56B (FY 2025). While this supports future production, it represents a significant cash commitment.

5. **PEG Ratio Signal**: The PEG ratio of 28.15 is unusually high, potentially signaling that the market expects growth to decelerate meaningfully or that earnings are near-cycle-peak.

6. **Concentration in Gold**: As a pure-play gold miner, there is no commodity diversification.
