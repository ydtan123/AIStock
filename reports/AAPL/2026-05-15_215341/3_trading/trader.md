**Reasoning Anchored in the Analyst Team’s Research Plan**

Key factors from the analysts’ reports driving the sell decision:

- **Stretched valuation:** AAPL trades at a forward P/E of 33.4x and a PEG of 2.57, discounting sustained high growth that the law of large numbers makes unlikely. - **Concentrated downside vs. minimal upside:** The plan’s upside target is $305 (~2% from recent levels), while the stop‑loss is set at $280 and the bear case implies a plunge to $170. The asymmetrical payoff strongly favors selling.

- **Scale out on strength:** Use any rally toward $305 as an opportunity to sell an additional tranche.
