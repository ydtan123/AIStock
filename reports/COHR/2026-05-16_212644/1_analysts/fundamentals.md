## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

Coherent Corp. (formerly Coherent, Inc.) is a global leader in lasers, laser-based technologies, and laser-based system solutions serving scientific, commercial, and industrial research applications. Headquartered in Saxonburg, Pennsylvania, the company trades on the NYSE under ticker COHR. Its fiscal year ends June 30. The company underwent a transformative merger with II-VI Incorporated in 2022, which dramatically expanded its scale, product breadth, and market reach across photonics, compound semiconductors, and advanced materials.

## 11. BOTTOM-LINE ASSESSMENT

Coherent Corp. is in the midst of a powerful operational turnaround following its transformative II-VI merger. Revenue growth is accelerating, margins are expanding, and debt is declining — all hallmarks of successful integration. The 73% YoY earnings growth and 20.5% revenue growth paint a picture of a company hitting its stride.

However, the Q3 FY2026 cash flow data introduces significant caution. A $537M quarterly FCF deficit driven by surging inventory and CapEx raises questions about working capital management and whether the company is over-investing ahead of demand. The stock's extreme trailing valuation (185.9x P/E) means that any stumble in the growth narrative could trigger a sharp re-rating.

The balance between momentum and risk is finely poised: the operational trends are excellent, but the valuation is stretched, and the cash flow anomaly in the latest quarter cannot be ignored. Traders should weigh the strong secular growth story against the near-term cash flow concerns and the fact that the stock has already rallied ~430% from its 52-week low.
