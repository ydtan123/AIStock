## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

Meta Platforms, Inc. (formerly Facebook, Inc.) is a global technology conglomerate headquartered at 1 Meta Way, Menlo Park, California. The company operates in the **Communication Services** sector within the **Internet Content & Information** industry. Its core products span social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp), virtual/augmented reality (Quest headsets, Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses), and increasingly, artificial intelligence infrastructure. The company employs a December fiscal year-end.

### 6.3 Capex Analysis — The AI Arms Race

This is arguably the most important trend for META investors. Capex has exploded:

- 2021: $18.6B
- 2022: $31.4B (+69%)
- 2023: $27.3B (-13%, pullback)
- 2024: $37.3B (+37%)

The Q1 2026 capex of $19.0B consumed 59% of operating cash flow. This is META going all-in on AI data centers, GPUs, and related infrastructure. Depreciation has also surged — $18.6B in FY2025 vs. $15.5B in FY2024 (+20%), and Q1 2026 D&A hit $6.0B (annualized $24B). This will increasingly weigh on reported earnings as these assets depreciate.

## 10. KEY RISKS & CONCERNS

1. **Massive Capex Cycle:** $70B+ annual capex on AI infrastructure with uncertain ROI. If AI monetization disappoints, these assets become stranded.
2. **Rising Depreciation:** D&A surged to $6.0B in Q1 2026 alone and will continue to rise as assets are placed in service, compressing margins.
3. **Debt Accumulation:** Total debt has grown 6x since 2021 to $86.8B. While net debt is minimal, gross leverage is rising.
4. **Regulatory Risk:** As a dominant player in social media and digital advertising, META faces ongoing antitrust and privacy regulation globally.
5. **Tax Rate Volatility:** Q3 2025 saw a massive $18.95B tax charge, and Q1 2026 had a -$5.0B tax benefit. Earnings are being distorted by irregular tax items.
6. **Ad Market Sensitivity:** Despite diversification, META remains heavily dependent on digital advertising (~98% of revenue).

## 11. KEY POSITIVE SIGNALS

1. **Accelerating Revenue Growth:** +33.1% YoY in Q1 2026, the fastest in years.
2. **Industry-Leading Margins:** 40%+ operating margins with 82% gross margins are virtually unmatched among mega-caps.
3. **PEG Below 1.0:** At 0.889, the stock appears undervalued on a growth-adjusted basis.
4. **Aggressive Buybacks:** 23% share count reduction over 5 years.
5. **Net-Debt-Neutral Balance Sheet:** $81B in liquid assets offsets virtually all debt.
6. **AI Monetization Potential:** META's massive user base (3B+ across platforms) provides unparalleled data for AI-driven ad targeting.
