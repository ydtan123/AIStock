## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

TechnipFMC PLC is a London-headquartered, NYSE-listed global leader in oil and gas projects, technologies, systems, and services. Operating in the **Energy / Oil & Gas Equipment & Services** sector, the company provides integrated solutions across subsea, onshore/offshore, and surface technologies. The company has undergone a significant transformation since its 2017 merger and subsequent restructuring, emerging as a leaner, more focused entity after divesting non-core assets and aggressively reducing debt.

## 8. KEY RISK FACTORS

1. **Cyclical Energy Exposure:** As an oil & gas services company, FTI remains exposed to commodity price cycles and upstream/downstream capex cycles. A sustained oil price decline could pressure order intake.

2. **Negative Retained Earnings:** At -$3.70B, retained deficit limits dividend flexibility and could constrain future capital returns if earnings reverse.

3. **Valuation Richness:** At 27x trailing P/E and 8.3x book, the stock already prices in significant optimism. Any earnings disappointment could lead to multiple contraction.

4. **Receivables Concentration:** Large project-based receivables ($2.76B) carry execution and collection risk, particularly in international markets.

5. **Capital Lease Burden:** $884M in capital lease obligations, though manageable against cash flows, represents fixed commitments that persist through cycles.

6. **Margin Sustainability:** The Q1 2026 operating margin of 14.1% is a peak. Sustaining double-digit margins through energy cycles remains unproven.
