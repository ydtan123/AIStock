# Comprehensive Fundamental Analysis Report: Agnico Eagle Mines Limited (AEM)
**Date:** June 1, 2026

## 1. Company Profile & Overview
**Agnico Eagle Mines Limited (AEM)** is a leading global gold mining company engaged in the exploration, development, and production of mineral properties primarily in Canada, Sweden, and Finland. Headquartered in Toronto, Canada, the company operates in the Basic Materials sector under the Gold industry. Agnico Eagle is known for its high-quality asset base, jurisdictional safety, and strong track record of operational execution.

## 3. Financial Statement Analysis & Trends

### **Income Statement Highlights**
AEM has demonstrated a very strong growth trajectory over the past year, likely benefiting from a favorable macroeconomic environment for gold and strong operational execution.
- **Revenue Growth:** Q1 2026 revenue was $4.10 Billion, an increase from Q4 2025 ($3.56 Billion) and Q1 2025 ($2.47 Billion). The Year-over-Year (YoY) quarterly revenue growth is a staggering 66.1%.
- **Profitability:** Gross profit for Q1 2026 stood at $2.72 Billion, yielding exceptional gross margins. Net Income in Q1 2026 was $1.69 Billion, up from $814 Million in Q1 2025. Quarterly earnings growth YoY is extremely high at 108.6%.
- **Margins:** Operating margin (TTM) is phenomenal at 62.8%, showcasing that AEM retains a large majority of its revenue as operating profit, which is top-tier for the capital-intensive mining industry.

### **Balance Sheet & Solvency**
AEM maintains a fortress balance sheet, positioning it perfectly for both defensive security and offensive capital deployment.
- **Liquidity:** As of March 31, 2026, the company holds $3.11 Billion in Cash and Cash Equivalents, up from $2.87 Billion at the end of 2025 and significantly up from early 2025. 
- **Assets & Liabilities:** Total Assets sit at $35.16 Billion against Total Liabilities of just $8.88 Billion, showcasing low leverage. 
- **Debt Profile:** The company's Long-Term Debt stands at a negligible $196.5 Million against its massive equity base of $26.27 Billion. This near-zero net debt position minimizes interest rate risk and allows for maximum flexibility.

### **Cash Flow Dynamics**
- **Operating Cash Flow:** Q1 2026 saw operating cash flow of $1.35 Billion. The total operating cash flow generated in FY 2025 was a massive $6.82 Billion.
- **Capital Expenditures (CapEx) & Free Cash Flow (FCF):** In Q1 2026, CapEx was $619 Million, leading to a Free Cash Flow of ~$726 Million. The company consistently generates robust FCF, comfortably funding its operations, exploration, and dividend payouts (Q1 2026 dividend payout was $203 Million).
