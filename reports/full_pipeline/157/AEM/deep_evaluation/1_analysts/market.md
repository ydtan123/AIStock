### I. Indicator Selection & Rationale

To formulate a comprehensive technical perspective for Agnico Eagle Mines (AEM) on June 1, 2026, a diverse set of 8 complementary indicators was carefully selected. These choices are designed to evaluate the stock's trend, momentum, volatility, and mean-reversion potential without introducing redundant signals:

1. **200 SMA (`close_200_sma`)**: Used as the ultimate benchmark for the primary long-term trend and major support/resistance.
2. **50 SMA (`close_50_sma`)**: Gauges the medium-term trend and helps map out potential pullbacks or shifting momentum structures.
3. **10 EMA (`close_10_ema`)**: Provides a highly responsive, short-term measure of price action, useful for spotting immediate trend shifts before longer-duration moving averages can react.
4. **MACD (`macd`)**: Measures trend strength and momentum divergence.
5. **MACD Histogram (`macdh`)**: Acts as a leading indicator within the MACD ecosystem to visualize momentum exhaustion and identify short-term trend reversals via signal line crossovers.
6. **RSI (`rsi`)**: Measures absolute momentum against extreme thresholds (overbought/oversold) to contextualize selling pressure. 
7. **Bollinger Upper Band (`boll_ub`)** & **8. Bollinger Lower Band (`boll_lb`)**: Measures volatility and potential structural exhaustion. These bands dynamically map where prices are statistically stretched, helping to filter false breakouts and identify high-probability reversal zones.
