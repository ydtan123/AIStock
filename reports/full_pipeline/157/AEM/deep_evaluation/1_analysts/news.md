*   **"Green Wall" Trade Escalation (May 28):** 
    The U.S. imposed "Strategic Autonomy" tariffs on third-party assembled EV components and Solid-State Batteries originating from Southeast Asia, aiming to block Chinese regulatory bypasses.
    *   *Trading Implication:* Bullish for North American and near-shored (Mexico) battery manufacturers, but highly inflationary for legacy automotive OEMs facing immediate supply chain re-routing costs.
*   **OPEC+ Production Cuts (May 31):** 
    OPEC+ proactively extended its 2.2 million bpd production cuts through the end of 2026 in response to weakening Chinese industrial demand (PMI < 49.0 for three consecutive months).
