## 1. Company Overview

Credo Technology Group Holding Ltd is a high-speed connectivity solutions provider focused on electrical and optical Ethernet applications. The company serves data center, AI/machine learning, enterprise networking, and telecommunications markets. Headquartered in San Jose, CA (incorporated in the Cayman Islands), with operations in the US, Mexico, Malaysia, and Hong Kong. Credo specializes in high-speed serializer/deserializer (SerDes) technology, active electrical cables (AEC), and optical DSP solutions critical for AI infrastructure buildouts.
